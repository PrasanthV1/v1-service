Frequently Asked Questions

What is V1 Services?
V1 Services is a structured Accounts & Finance service platform that connects clients with verified professionals using a clear, trackable process.
What is V1Books?
V1Books is the digital platform used by V1 Services to manage requests, documents, tracking, and continuity of work.
Can I work with the same auditor every month?
Yes. For recurring services like GST and compliance, we ensure auditor continuity to maintain consistency and data safety.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Your documents and information are shared only with assigned professionals and are stored securely.
What services do you provide?
We cover all major Accounts & Finance activities including GST, Income Tax, TDS, registrations, compliance, and statutory notices.
How do I track my request?
Every request generates a Tracking ID, which allows you to track status at any time.
